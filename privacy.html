Privacy Policy — BandoIQ

BandoIQ does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.

Setup sheets you create are stored locally on your device only, and only if you enable the "Save setups" option in the app. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to Erin Brandt Motorsports or any third party.

BandoIQ uses Apple's StoreKit to process an optional annual subscription. Payment information is handled entirely by Apple and is never seen by Erin Brandt Motorsports.

BandoIQ does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies of any kind.
